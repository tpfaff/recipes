Preheat oven to 450F 
1.25C tapioca flour in a mixing bowl
mix in 1C shredded mozzarella, 1t baking powder. Get those mixed, crack 2 eggs in and mixed. 
Melt 0.25C butter and mix in. Throw 2t basil and 2 t oregano in. 
Sprinkle tapioca flour and roll around till you have a ball you can hold. 
Lightly oil the skilled and spread it on. 
Add your sauce (we just use flavorful pasta sauce), shredded mozzarella, and toppings. 
Heat cast iron skillet with pizza on high for 2-3min, throw in oven ~12min. 
Pull out, slide onto plate, cool a little and enjoy!
